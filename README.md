An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.  
The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making by transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights.

────────────────────────────────────
Project Overview

This dashboard delivers a full analytical view of business performance across regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping methods.  
It helps stakeholders identify growth opportunities, monitor KPIs, and optimize revenue strategies.

────────────────────────────────────
Tools & Technologies

• Power BI  
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
• Data Modeling (Star Schema Approach)  
• Data Visualization & Storytelling  

────────────────────────────────────
Key Metrics

• Total Sales Performance: 1.4M+ peak revenue  
• Profit Analysis: Tracked across all regions & categories  
• Customer Segments: Corporate, Consumer, Home Office, Small Business  
• Regional Coverage: Ontario, West, Quebec, Atlantic  
• Shipping Modes: Regular, Delivery, Express  
• Time Intelligence: Daily & Monthly trend analysis  

────────────────────────────────────
Key Insights

• Ontario leads all regions with the highest sales (~0.35M+)  
• Strong seasonal peaks observed in January & September  
• Corporate segment dominates total revenue (~36%+)  
• Regular shipping is the most preferred (~50%+)  
• Sales volatility indicates strong mid-month performance spikes
