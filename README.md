🧠 Project Overview
───────────────────────────────────────

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.

It transforms raw sales data into actionable insights to support **data-driven decision-making** and improve business strategy.

───────────────────────────────────────

🛠 Tools & Technologies
───────────────────────────────────────

• Power BI  
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
• Data Modeling (Star Schema Approach)  
• Data Visualization & Storytelling  

───────────────────────────────────────

📈 Key Metrics
───────────────────────────────────────

• Total Sales Performance: 1.4M+ peak revenue  
• Profit Analysis: Tracked across all regions & categories  
• Customer Segments: Corporate, Consumer, Home Office, Small Business  
• Regional Coverage: Ontario, West, Quebec, Atlantic  
• Shipping Modes: Regular, Delivery, Express  
• Time Intelligence: Daily & Monthly trend analysis  

───────────────────────────────────────

🔎 Key Insights
───────────────────────────────────────

• Ontario leads all regions with the highest sales (~0.35M+)  
• Strong seasonal peaks in January & September  
• Corporate segment contributes the largest share (~36%+)  
• Regular shipping dominates usage (~50%+)  
• Sales show clear mid-month performance spikes
