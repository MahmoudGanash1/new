The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights that support strategic business decisions.

═══════════════════════════════════════
PROJECT OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════

This dashboard provides a full view of business performance across regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping modes.

It helps stakeholders:
- Identify revenue drivers  
- Track performance trends  
- Optimize sales strategy  
- Improve profitability  

═══════════════════════════════════════
TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling (Star Schema Approach)  
- Data Visualization & Storytelling  

═══════════════════════════════════════
KEY METRICS
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Total Sales: 1.4M+ peak revenue  
- Profit Tracking: Across all regions & categories  
- Customer Segments: Corporate, Consumer, Home Office, Small Business  
- Regions: Ontario, West, Quebec, Atlantic  
- Shipping Modes: Regular, Delivery, Express  
- Time Analysis: Daily & Monthly trends  

═══════════════════════════════════════
KEY INSIGHTS
═══════════════════════════════════════

- Ontario is the top-performing region (~0.35M+)  
- Sales peak in January and September  
- Corporate segment leads revenue (~36%+)  
- Regular shipping dominates usage (~50%+)  
- Mid-month shows strong sales spikes
